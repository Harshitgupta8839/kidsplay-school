OUR FOUNDER

Abhinav Upmanyu

In 2021, Abhinav Upmanyu founded kids play school in delhi for caring for and educating pre-school children whose parents were absent during the day. In an age when school was restricted to children who had already learned to read and write at home, there were many attempts to make school accessible to orphans or to the children of women who worked in factories.This school was founded.
